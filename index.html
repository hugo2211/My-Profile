About Me

Quick Bio

Hi, my full name is Victor Hugo Mendizabal Campos. My family and close friends call me Hugo.

I was born in Santa Cruz, Bolivia. I am the oldest of three and the only boy. My family decided to move to the United Sates when I was four years old. We first lived in New York city, but a year later we found ourselves in Texas. I've lived in the DFW area for most of my life.

Thanks to being born in a different country, I grew up bi-lingual. I speak English and Spanish. I also enjoy trying to learn new languages. I can get by understanding Portuguese, Italian, and French, but I can't speak or write in them. I do want to eventually learn Japannese so I can watch anime without sub titles.

My current goal in life is to become a software developer and to buy my first home. I also love sports, every Dallas team except the Rangers. I enjoy hiking, movies, anime, playing soccer, and traveling.
